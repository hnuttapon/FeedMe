Objective


-To encourage people to do more exercise with hapiness.

-To encourage people to care more about their health.

-To make more friends.

-To reduce amout of people suffer from diseases for example cardiovascular disease and etc.

Background


In this modern world Era, people turn to take care themselves in doing exercises, but problem is sometimes people are lazy to do. Even there are many types of doing exercise, we focus on running because it is easy to do without using any sports equipment while running, using only your own effort, and runners also go outside to meet other people. We took advantage from this issue that people want to do exercise along with have fun. So, we decided to do the game for everyone who were bored indoor exercise so that people will have fun while running.

Scope


-Android device only.

-Scope within Chiangmai University.

-Multiplayer mode allow only 5 people per room.

-There will be 2 avatars for players and they can change their cloth.

-Players will be able feed their pet and also change their dress.
