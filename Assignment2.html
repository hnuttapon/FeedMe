246492 HCI Project Proposal


The first paragraph identifies the problem that motivates you to do this project. You should convince the readers of your proposal to agree with you that the problem is interesting, and the level of difficulty is appropriate for you to invest time throughout the whole semester to do this project. Clearly write about the problem. What it is. The second paragraph analyzes the problem by giving background and surrounding information. Describe a scenario which the problem occurs, and how it can be resolved. The existing systems should be analyzed to support your writing. So that readers know limitations of existing solutions. The third paragraph suggests how technology could be used to solve the problem.
